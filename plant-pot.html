Plant Pot
The plant pot is an everyday item that holds a plant. It is used to keep the plant housed when not planted in the ground. It helps to keep mess to a minimum and is often made out of plain terracotta. They may also be made of plastic, metal, wood, stone,or even biodegradable paper.
